# Adidas US Sales Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Business Intelligence dashboard built using Microsoft Power BI to analyze the sales performance of Adidas across different regions, retailers, product categories, and sales channels in the United States.

The dashboard provides clear insights into revenue, profit, and sales trends, helping stakeholders understand business performance and make data-driven decisions.

## Key Business Metrics

The dashboard highlights important Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):

* **Total Sales:** $900M
* **Units Sold:** 2 Million
* **Operating Profit:** $332M
* **Price per Unit:** $45
* **Operating Margin:** 42%

These KPIs provide a quick overview of the company's financial and operational performance.

---

## Dashboard Features

### Sales Method Analysis

This section shows the distribution of sales across different sales channels:

* Online
* Outlet
* In-store

It helps identify which sales channel contributes the most to total revenue.

### Region Wise Sales

The dashboard compares sales performance across multiple regions:

* West
* Northeast
* South
* Southeast
* Midwest

This analysis helps identify high-performing markets and potential growth areas.

### Retailer Performance

The dashboard analyzes sales generated by major retailers including:

* West Gear
* Foot Locker
* Sports Direct
* Kohl's
* Amazon
* Walmart

This insight helps understand which partners generate the most revenue.

### Product Category Analysis

This section highlights the best-selling product categories such as:

* Men's Street Footwear
* Women's Apparel
* Men's Athletic Footwear
* Women's Street Footwear
* Men's Apparel

It helps identify customer demand and popular product segments.

### Monthly Sales Trend

A time-series visualization shows sales performance across each month of the year.
This helps identify:

* Seasonal trends
* Peak sales months
* Revenue fluctuations

### Geographic Sales Visualization

An interactive map displays sales distribution across the United States, helping identify regions with strong or weak performance.

## Interactive Filters

The dashboard includes slicers that allow dynamic analysis:

* **Region Filter**
* **Invoice Date Filter**

Users can explore the data by selecting different regions and time periods.
